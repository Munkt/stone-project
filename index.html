The Ascent

Have you ever climbed a website?
The Ascent is a refuge in the chaotic digital world. Like escaping to the mountains, this site offers a break from the endless cycle of apps and services that consume our attention with ads, recommendations, and notifications. Sometimes we need a space for pure fun and relaxation, free from productivity or utility.

The function is simple:
as you ascend, collect stones and stack them at the peak with a small message. The form is entirely up to you, even if it defies gravity. This is a space where you can choose, create freely, and loosely connect with others.
